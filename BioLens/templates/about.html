About BioLens

BioLens is an interactive web tool designed to help students, educators, and enthusiasts engage with biological cell diagrams in a new dimension. Using advanced image recognition and AI, BioLens analyzes your uploaded diagram and visualizes its corresponding cell in 3D!

Features

  • Automatic Diagram Identification: Upload a labeled cell diagram and let BioLens detect its type automatically.
  • Structure & Function Insight: The system extracts structural and functional information about the detected cell type.
  • 3D Model Visualization: Instantly view a detailed 3D model of the predicted cell type directly in your browser.
  • Text Extraction (CLIP): BioLens reads and reports text found in your image.
  • Confidence Scoring: Find out how certain the system is about its prediction.

How It Works

  1. Click "Analyze Images" after uploading one or more biology diagrams.
  2. The system uses AI (Google Cloud Vision + custom models) to extract text and recognize the diagram.
  3. You receive a 3D visualization, analysis, structure, function, and a report about your diagrams.
